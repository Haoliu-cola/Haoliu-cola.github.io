I’m a fifth year Ph.D. candidate at Washington University in St. Louis. I’m fortunately advised by Dr. Yixin Chen. Prior to joining WashU, I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Mathematics from Beijing Normal University. My research primarily focuses on addressing few-shot and zero-shot challenges in graph and tabular domains. Additionally, I have a keen interest in applying these to real-world clinical problems. Please check out my resume here: Resume.

My recent interest is to design a graph foundation model leveraging LLMs. Please check our recent work here: OneForAll.

I am actively seeking a summer internship for 2024. Happy to chat about potential opportunities and experiences!
